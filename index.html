Directions

We currently hold our events at the Uganda Industrial Research Institute (UIRI) Conference Center in Nakawa. Follow the map or direction below:

  • • If coming from Nakawa towards Kyambogo, after the Spear Motors Junction, after City Oil Service Station you'll see a Pepsi Bilboard on your right.
  • • Turn right at the bilboard and drive up to the Pepsi Depot
  • • The UIRI Conference Center gate is on the left, next to the Pepsi Depot. Follow the music!
